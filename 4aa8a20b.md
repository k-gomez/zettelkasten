# Asymmetric
Parent: [[83a39c63]]

If individual x is related to individual y then individual y is not related to individual x along the same property.

Inverse of [[3f12703b]]

## Example

- `A isNotConnected B` than `B isNotConnected A` if `isNotConnected` is asymmetric
